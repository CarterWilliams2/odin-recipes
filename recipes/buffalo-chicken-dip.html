Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

Creamy and spicy chicken dip recipe that goes great with anything you like.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 2 cups cooked chicken shredded
  • 1/2 cup Frank's Original Red Hot Sauce
  • 8 oz block cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/2 cup white Cheddar cheese freshly shredded
  • 1/4 cup crumbled blue cheese
  • 2 teaspoons green onions, sliced
  • Tortilla chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. Arrange oven rack to the middle of your oven.
  2. In an 8-inch cast-iron skillet, melt the butter over medium-high heat. Saute garlic until fragant. Add the chicken and hot sauce and simmer until sauce has thickened and reduced by half.
  3. Reduce heat to low and stir in cream cheese; mix until combined. Take off heat, stir through sour cream and top with both cheddar cheese over the top.
  4. Bake until bubbling around the edges and the cheese has melted. Broil for a further minute to brown on top.
  5. Imediately garnish with blue cheese and green onions. Serve with chips
