Crack Chicken

Description

This dish is a creamy chicken mix with bacon and cheese. It goes great on a sandwich bun and can be served with barbeque sauce.

Ingredients

  • Chicken thighs
  • Ranch dressing seasoning mix
  • Cream Cheese
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Cooked Bacon
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Add chicken to the slow cooker
  2. Top with the ranch packet and cream cheese. Wait to add other ingredients
  3. Place the lid on the slow cooker
  4. Cook on low for 7 hours and do not open the lid during the cooking time
  5. After the cooking time is up, shred the chicken with 2 forks
  6. Add the cooked bacon then top with the cheddar cheese. Place the lid back on to let the cheese melt. Sprinkle over green onions if desired
